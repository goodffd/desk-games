Subset created with pyftsubset (fonttools) for desk-games project.
Source files:
  - noto-sans-sc-chinese-simplified-400-normal.woff2 (Chinese Simplified block)
  - noto-sans-sc-latin-400-normal.woff2 (Latin block)
Merged and subsetted to cover 358 CJK unified ideographs used as fixed UI
text in the app, plus U+0020-007E (basic Latin: card ranks 3-10/J/Q/K/A,
digits, common punctuation).
